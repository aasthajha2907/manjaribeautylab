What You'll Learn

The Curriculum

01

Hair — Foundation to Advanced

  • Hair anatomy and scalp science
  • Precision cutting techniques — layers, fringes, one-length
  • Blow-drying, crimping and heat styling
  • Braiding and up-style techniques
  • Hair colouring — root touch-up, global colour, highlights
  • Chemical treatments — Olaplex, rebonding, keratin
  • Hair spa and scalp treatments
  • Dandruff and hair fall management
02

Skin — Basics to Clinical

  • Skin anatomy, types and conditions
  • Clean-up and facial techniques
  • Professional facial products — O3+, L'Oreal, Kenpeki
  • Bleach application — face and body
  • Chemical peels — glycolic, lactic, azelaic
  • Anti-ageing and brightening protocols
  • Acne management and hydration therapy
  • Sheet masks and skin boosters
  • Customised treatment planning
03

Makeup — Party to Bridal

  • Colour theory and skin tone matching
  • Base preparation and application
  • Eye makeup — natural, smokey and glitter
  • Contouring, highlighting and setting
  • Party makeup — light, HD and glam
  • Bridal makeup — engagement, reception and HD bridal
  • Lash application
  • Saree draping and dupatta setting
  • Client consultation and look planning
04

Nails — Polish to Extensions

  • Nail anatomy and hygiene protocols
  • Manicure and pedicure basics
  • Gel polish — application and removal
  • Nail extensions — basic, gel and acrylic
  • Extension refills and safe removal
  • Nail art — simple, glitter, stone, marble, cartoon
  • Bridal nail design and styling
05

Business & Client Skills

  • Salon setup and management basics
  • Client consultation techniques
  • Pricing and service menu creation
  • Social media for beauty professionals
  • Building a personal client base
  • Professional ethics and hygiene standards
