Meet Your Cosmetologist

Manjari Jha

Most people walk into a salon knowing something is wrong — their hair keeps breaking, their skin won't clear up, their scalp is a mess — but they've never had anyone actually sit down and figure out why.

That's Manjari's speciality. In a single consultation, she can diagnose the root cause of your hair or skin concerns, map out what's actually going on, and recommend a treatment plan that addresses the problem — not just the symptom. No guesswork, no generic facials, no one-size-fits-all. A real plan, built for you.

◈ Root cause diagnosis for hair fall, dandruff and scalp conditions
◈ Personalised skin treatment plans for acne, pigmentation and ageing
◈ Custom treatment regimes you can actually maintain at home
◈ Honest recommendations — she'll tell you what you need, not what's most expensive
