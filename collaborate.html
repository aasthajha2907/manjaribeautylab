For Institutes & Academies

Visiting Faculty

Manjari is available as a visiting faculty member for beauty schools, cosmetology institutes and training centres looking to bring in a working practitioner for speciality modules.

What she can cover:

  • Advanced bridal makeup techniques
  • Chemical peels and clinical skin treatments
  • Olaplex and bond-repair hair therapy
  • Nail extension and nail art masterclasses
  • Client consultation and treatment planning
  • Any speciality module from her full curriculum

Sessions can be tailored to your institute's curriculum requirements. One-day workshops, multi-session modules or full course support — all open for discussion.

For Events & Organisers

Event Sponsorship & Styling

Manjari's Beauty Lab partners with event organisers to provide professional hair and makeup for models, participants and performers. If you're hosting a fashion show, cultural event, college fest or brand activation, we'd love to be part of it.

What we offer:

  • On-site hair and makeup for models and participants
  • Backstage beauty team for fashion shows
  • Grooming and styling for anchors and performers
  • Branding and sponsorship visibility at the event
  • Promotional collaboration on social media

Open to college events, cultural programmes, NGO initiatives, brand activations and more. Let's talk about what makes sense for your event.
